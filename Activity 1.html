Facts about Dogs

Dogs are descendants of wolves – Domestic dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) evolved from wolves thousands of years ago through domestication. There are over 340 recognized dog breeds – These range from tiny Chihuahuas to giant Great Danes.

A dog’s sense of smell is incredible – It's 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans'. That’s why dogs are used in search and rescue, drug detection, and even detecting medical conditions like cancer or seizures.
